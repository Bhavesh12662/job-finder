Privacy policy

Clear, simple privacy information for this platform.

This page explains what information is used by the platform, how it is stored, and what control users have over their data in this demo experience.

1. Introduction

Job Finder Platform is a simple web interface for browsing job listings and keeping track of saved or applied roles. This demo is designed to minimize personal data handling and keep the experience transparent.

2. Data Usage

The platform does not require account creation for its core features. In this version, the main user data stored in the browser is related to job preferences such as saved jobs and applied jobs.

Important: saved and applied job selections are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. They are not submitted to a backend in this frontend experience.

3. Storage

Saved job IDs and applied job IDs may be stored on your device through local browser storage so that your dashboard state remains available between visits. No password, billing data, or account profile information is collected by these static pages.

4. User Rights

Because the demo stores preference data locally, you remain in direct control of that information. You can clear your browser storage at any time to remove saved and applied job states from this device.

  • You can choose not to use the save or apply-tracking features.
  • You can clear localStorage through your browser settings whenever you want.
  • You can contact the platform owner with privacy questions through the Contact page.

5. Policy Scope

This policy applies to the current demo interface and may be updated if future versions add backend services, analytics, user authentication, or additional storage mechanisms.
